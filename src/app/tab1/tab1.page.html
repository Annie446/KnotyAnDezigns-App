Welcome to Knoty An Dezigns!

Product Page Review Page

Knoty An Dezigns founded by Anna A. Reece, is a registered business in Saint Lucia ad of December 18, 2020. Originally the business made only friendship bracelet styled jewelry. Ranging from chokers to bracelets to rings to waist chains and anklets. In 2018 and 2019, Anna decided she wanted to expand her product line rather than limiting herself to only friendship bracelet styled jewelry. This is when she discovered resin. With her science background she was intrigued. With failure after failure she finally figured out how to make unique resin pieces. To kick off this now product line, our resin keychains were introduced to the public in 2020. At the point there refinement of the craft was needed. A soft launch was done to test the receptiveness of current customers and the potential market diaspora. Once released the initial keychains received a positive response confirming the addition to our product line. Ever since this introduction, Knoty An Dezigns has increased its resin product line as they now offer a variety of keychains, lash trays, ash trays, 6 inch letter with and without lights, resin pour wall art, pens, books, bookmarks, dominos, boxes, self defense tools,paper weights, necklaces and more. Now that we have grown in the field of resin, our founder had an idea of adding plastic into our creation. She has a passion for Marine life conservation. She knows that apart from toxic chemicals released into our waterways, that plastic, our garbage, has a major impact on our marine eco system. She believes that no matter how small, an action taken to reduce our addition plastic will make a difference. She wants to make a difference and is now looking into creating products that no only add a smile to our customers but are also sustainable. The future of Knoty An Dezigns is turning waste into wonder, while spreading love and joy to customers. We blend creativity and sustainability to produce eco-friendly designs
